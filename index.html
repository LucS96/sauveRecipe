Mozzarella Sticks



I like mozzarella sticks because they are quick, easy, and delicious. these are great for a quick snack to help get me through the day. with these simple instructions and minimal ingredients you can enjoy these cheesy treats yourself in no time.

Ingedients

  • ¼ cup of flour
  • 1 cup italian styled breadcrums
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese, cut into ¾ inch x ¾ inch strips (or you can use string cheese and cut each in half)
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup jarred pizza sauce or 1 cup marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs and milk together.
  2. Coat each piece of cheese with flour, then dip in egg and then roll in bread crumbs. Dip in egg and crumbs again.
  3. Freeze before frying.
  4. Heat oil in skillet and cook sticks for about a minute on each side, or until golden and before cheese starts to leak.
  5. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve with sauce for dipping.
